## 📂 Course Structure & Topics

The following list outlines the major topics covered and the associated assignments (**Tugasan 1 - 19**).
Note that, the 'Learning & Teaching' was done in Malay. So his notes were also prepared in Malay. Tugasan means Assignment. 

### 0. Computing Foundations (Latar Pengiraan Berkomputer)
* Introduction to numerical methods and computer architecture.
* **Assignment:** Tugasan 1

### 1. Ordinary Differential Equations (ODE)
* Initial, boundary, and eigenvalue problems.
* Implementations: Leapfrog (Lompat Katak) and Runge-Kutta (RK4) methods for particle dynamics and chaotic systems.
* **Assignments:** Tugasan 2, 3, 4

### 2. Matrix Computation
* Matrix solutions and linear algebra in physics.
* **Assignment:** Tugasan 5

### 3. Partial Differential Equations (PDE)
* Elliptic, Parabolic, and Hyperbolic equations.
* **Assignment:** Tugasan 6

### 4. Image and Graphics
* Representation, transformation, and image processing techniques.
* **Assignments:** Tugasan 7, 8

### 5. Probabilistic Methods
* Random numbers, Monte Carlo simulations, and the Metropolis Algorithm.
* **Assignments:** Tugasan 9, 10, 11

### 6. Statistical Analysis
* Probability distributions and parameter estimations.
* **Assignment:** Tugasan 12

### 7. Signal Processing & Pattern Recognition
* Handling noise, signal processing, and clustering.
* **Assignment:** Tugasan 13

### 8. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Methods
* Search strategies, symbolic manipulation, and machine learning (Induction/Learning).
* **Assignments:** Tugasan 14, 15

### 9. Data and Information
* Big Data handling, information retrieval, and data visualization.
* **Assignment:** Tugasan 16

### 10. Data Security
* Encryption and Blockchain technology.
* **Assignment:** Tugasan 17

### 11. Hardware & Architecture
* Cores, coprocessors, and parallel/distributed systems.
* **Assignment:** Tugasan 18

### 12. Quantum Computing
* Quantum operations, algorithms, and hardware fundamentals.
* **Assignment:** Tugasan 19

## 🛠️ Requirements

Most of the simulations are written in **Python 3** and require the following libraries:

* **NumPy**: For numerical arrays and matrix operations.
* **Matplotlib**: For plotting trajectories, phase plots, and animations.
* **SciPy**: For advanced ODE solvers and integration.
